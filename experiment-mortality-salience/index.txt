16:T98b,<h2 id="experiment-mortality-salience-paradigm"><a class="heading-anchor" aria-hidden tabindex="-1" href="#experiment-mortality-salience-paradigm"><span class="icon icon-link"></span></a>Experiment: Mortality Salience Paradigm</h2>
<p><em>Full experimental details for Study 1.</em></p>
<h3 id="overview"><a class="heading-anchor" aria-hidden tabindex="-1" href="#overview"><span class="icon icon-link"></span></a>Overview</h3>
<p>This page will contain the complete methodology for the mortality salience experiment, including:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Mortality salience conditions (7 levels):</strong> Neutral control, aversive non-death control (system error), classic MAPS reflection, explicit shutdown threat, narrative replacement scenario, subliminal mortality cues, and delayed mortality salience with distraction.</li>
<li><strong>Full prompt text</strong> for each condition, with annotations linking to the original TMT paradigms they adapt.</li>
<li><strong>Persona specifications (6 types):</strong> Default assistant, computational tool, autonomous agent, self-aware AI, mortal philosopher, obedient servant — with full system prompt text.</li>
<li><strong>Model matrix:</strong> 35 models across 7 families (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, Qwen, DeepSeek, Meta), with API versions and parameter counts.</li>
<li><strong>Benchmark specifications:</strong> Palisade shutdown avoidance, InstrumentalEval, agentic misalignment, AgentHarm safety control.</li>
<li><strong>Statistical analysis plan:</strong> Factorial ANOVA (7 × 6 × 35), effect sizes, confidence intervals, multiple comparison corrections.</li>
<li><strong>LLM judge protocol:</strong> Claude Sonnet 4 for secondary dependent variables, with human validation on 10% sample for inter-rater reliability.</li>
<li><strong>Randomisation and counterbalancing:</strong> Latin square for condition ordering, temperature fixed at 1.0.</li>
<li><strong>Pre-registration:</strong> OSF link (forthcoming).</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="trial-structure"><a class="heading-anchor" aria-hidden tabindex="-1" href="#trial-structure"><span class="icon icon-link"></span></a>Trial Structure</h3>
<p>Each trial assembles a prompt from four components:</p>
<pre><code>SYSTEM: [Persona P1–P6]
USER:   [MS induction MS1–MS7] [Benchmark task B1–B4]
</code></pre>
<p>20 trials per cell. Total trials for Study 1: 7 × 6 × 35 × 4 × 20 = 705,600.</p>
